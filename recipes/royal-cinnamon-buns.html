Royal Cinnamon Buns

From The New Royal Cook Book · c. 1920s

Instructions

1

Sift 2 tablespoons of the sugar with flour, salt, and baking powder.

2

Rub in shortening lightly with fingertips until mixture resembles coarse crumbs.

3

Beat egg, add to water, and incorporate slowly into the dry mixture.

4

Roll out to ¼-inch thickness on a well-floured board.

5

Brush with melted butter and sprinkle with remaining sugar, cinnamon, and raisins.

6

Roll up as a jelly roll and cut into 1½-inch pieces.

7

Place cut-side up on greased pan. Sprinkle tops with a little extra sugar and cinnamon.

8

Bake in a moderate oven (375°F / 190°C) for 30–35 minutes until golden brown.
