Pumpkin Fanchonettes

From American Cookery · November 1921

Instructions

1

Beat eggs and sugar together until light and fluffy.

2

Fold in pumpkin puree, vanilla, and spices until well combined.

3

Fill prepared pastry shells with the pumpkin mixture.

4

Bake at moderate heat (350°F / 175°C) until filling sets, about 25–30 minutes.
