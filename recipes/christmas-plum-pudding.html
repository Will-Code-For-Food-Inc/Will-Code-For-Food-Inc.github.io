Christmas Plum Pudding

From The New Royal Cook Book · c. 1920s

Instructions

1

Mix all dry ingredients thoroughly — flour, bread crumbs, sugar, baking powder, salt, and spices.

2

Add the ground suet and mix until evenly distributed.

3

Add all fruits — raisins, currants, citron, figs, and peels — and toss to coat.

4

Stir in water and fruit juice until forming a stiff dough.

5

Fill greased molds two-thirds full. Cover tightly.

6

Steam for 5–6 hours. The pudding will be very dark and dense.

7

Before serving, steam one additional hour to warm through. Turn out and top with sauce.
